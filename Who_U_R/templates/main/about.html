About Who U R

Discover the story behind our personality analysis platform and the developer who created it

About This Project

Who U R is an innovative personality analysis platform that provides deep insights into individual personality traits based on psychological principles. Our mission is to help people better understand themselves and others through advanced AI-powered analysis.

How It Works

The system uses sophisticated algorithms and natural language processing to generate personalized personality profiles. By analyzing the combination of name, birth date, and birthplace, our AI creates a comprehensive psychological portrait.

Technology Stack

  • Backend Django, Python
  • Frontend Tailwind CSS, JavaScript
  • AI Groq API, Meta-llama
